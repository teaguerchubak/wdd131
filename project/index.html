Who is Teaguer Chubak?

I’ve been involved in the SLC punk community since 2007. Over the years, I’ve volunteered and worked in just about every aspect of live music: from booking shows in venues ranging from underground punk houses to large theaters, to recording solo artists and full bands in a professional recording studio, to playing on TV and radio.
